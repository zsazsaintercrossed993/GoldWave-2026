GoldWave-2026 allows you to remove silence, trim sections, and apply effects to your recording.

Use your mouse to click and drag across the waveform. This highlights the section you want to change. The highlighted area turns a different color. You can cut, copy, or paste this selection using the Edit menu at the top of the window.

### Removing Silence
When you leave an long pause in your recording, you can remove it. Select the empty space in the waveform. Press the Delete key on your keyboard to remove that chunk of sound from the file.
